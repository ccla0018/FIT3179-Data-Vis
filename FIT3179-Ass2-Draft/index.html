World Freedom: 2010 - 2020

Freedom Index around the World



Accross 2010 - 2020, the World Human and Economic Freedom Index is a comprehensive and influential measure that assesses the degree of freedom enjoyed by individuals and businesses in countries around the world. Countries with high scores on the World Human and Economic Freedom Index are

often characterised by strong protections for individual rights, minimal government interference in economic activities, and vibrant free markets. Conversely, nations with lower scores may face challenges related to corruption, excessive regulations, and restrictions on personal freedoms.
